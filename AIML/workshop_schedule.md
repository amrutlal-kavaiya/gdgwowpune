# GDG WOW Pune - AI/ML Workshop Series 2025

This document outlines our planned AI/ML workshops and events for 2025. The curriculum is designed to be inclusive and accessible to women at all stages of their tech journey.

## Workshop Formats

- **Beginner Bootcamps**: 2-3 hour sessions introducing fundamental concepts
- **Hands-on Labs**: 4-hour practical sessions with coding exercises
- **Weekend Intensives**: Full-day deep dives into specific technologies
- **Expert Talks**: 1-hour sessions with industry leaders
- **Project Hackathons**: Collaborative events to build real-world applications
