## About GDG WOW Pune

Google Developer Group Women in Tech (GDG WOW) Pune is a community-driven initiative focused on empowering women in technology across Pune, India. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where women developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts can learn, share knowledge, and grow together.

## Recent Event: GDG WOW Summit 2025

We recently concluded our flagship 2-day GDG WOW Summit on April 15-16, 2025, at the Pune Tech Hub. The event was a tremendous success with over 500 attendees participating in workshops, panel discussions, and networking sessions.

### Event Highlights

- **Day 1**: Focused on cutting-edge technologies including AI/ML, Cloud Computing, and Mobile Development
- **Day 2**: Deep dive into career development, leadership skills, and entrepreneurship in tech

### Featured Speakers

We were honored to host an impressive lineup of speakers who shared their expertise and inspired our community:

- **Dr. Priya Sharma** - AI Research Lead at Google, who presented groundbreaking work on responsible AI development
- **Anjali Mehta** - CTO of CloudTech Solutions, discussing cloud architecture best practices
- **Neha Desai** - Founder & CEO of TechWomen Initiative, sharing insights on building inclusive tech companies
- **Vikram Singh** - Engineering Director at Google, conducting an interactive workshop on Flutter development
- **Dr. Meera Patel** - Professor of Computer Science, leading a panel on the future of women in STEM education
- **Kiran Kumar** - Senior Developer Advocate, guiding participants through hands-on Firebase implementations

### Workshop Sessions

Attendees participated in interactive workshops covering:
- Machine Learning with TensorFlow
- Cloud Architecture on Google Cloud Platform
- Mobile App Development with Flutter
- Web Development with Modern Frameworks
- Career Development and Technical Interview Preparation

### Community Impact

The event brought together professionals from various backgrounds and experience levels, creating meaningful connections and opportunities for collaboration. Multiple hiring partners also participated, offering career opportunities to talented attendees.

- Promote diversity and inclusion in the tech industry
