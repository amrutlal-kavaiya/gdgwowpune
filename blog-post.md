# GDG WOW Pune: Empowering Women in Technology

## Introduction

Google Developer Group (GDG) Women of Worth (WOW) Pune is a community-driven initiative focused on empowering women in technology. Operating as a specialized chapter of the broader GDG ecosystem, GDG WOW Pune creates inclusive spaces where women technologists can learn, connect, and grow together. The community serves as a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and professional development specifically tailored to address the unique challenges and opportunities faced by women in tech.

## Our Mission

At GDG WOW Pune, our mission is to:

- Foster a supportive community for women in technology
- Provide accessible learning opportunities across various technology domains
- Promote diversity and inclusion in the tech ecosystem
- Showcase and celebrate women's achievements in technology
- Create mentorship and networking opportunities for career advancement

## Events and Activities

Throughout the year, GDG WOW Pune organizes various events designed to educate, inspire, and connect women in technology:

### Technical Workshops
Hands-on sessions covering cutting-edge technologies like Flutter, Kotlin, Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, and Web Development. These workshops cater to different skill levels from beginners to advanced developers.

### Speaker Sessions
Talks by industry experts who share their knowledge and experiences. These sessions often feature prominent women in tech who serve as role models for the community.

### Hackathons
Collaborative coding events where participants work in teams to build solutions for real-world problems, fostering creativity and technical skills.

### Networking Meetups
Casual gatherings that facilitate connections among members, creating opportunities for mentorship, collaboration, and career growth.

### Annual WOW Conference
The flagship event bringing together hundreds of women technologists for a day of learning, inspiration, and networking. The conference features keynote talks, panel discussions, and interactive sessions on diverse tech topics.
