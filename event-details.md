# GDG WOW Summit 2025 - Detailed Event Information

## Event Overview

The GDG WOW Summit 2025 was a 2-day technical conference held at the Pune Tech Hub on April 15-16, 2025. The event brought together technology enthusiasts, industry professionals, and students interested in Google technologies and career advancement in the tech field.

## Day 1: Technical Deep Dives (April 15, 2025)

### Morning Sessions

#### Opening Keynote
**Dr. Priya Sharma** delivered an inspiring keynote on "The Future of Responsible AI," highlighting the importance of ethical considerations in artificial intelligence development. Her presentation showcased Google's latest approaches to building fair, accountable, and transparent AI systems.

#### Technical Workshops (Parallel Tracks)
1. **TensorFlow Workshop**: Led by **Vikram Singh**, this hands-on session guided participants through building and deploying machine learning models.
   
2. **Flutter Development**: A comprehensive workshop by **Neha Desai** demonstrating cross-platform app development with Flutter.

### Afternoon Sessions

#### Panel Discussion: "Cloud Innovations"
Moderated by **Anjali Mehta**, this panel brought together cloud architects and engineers to discuss the latest advancements in cloud technologies, focusing on Google Cloud Platform solutions for enterprise applications.

#### Hands-on Labs
1. **Firebase Implementation**: **Kiran Kumar** led attendees through real-time database implementation and authentication workflows.
   
2. **Android Development**: A practical session on building modern Android applications with Jetpack Compose.

#### Networking Evening
The day concluded with a networking session where attendees could connect with speakers and industry professionals while enjoying refreshments.

## Day 2: Career Development & Leadership (April 16, 2025)

### Morning Sessions

#### Keynote: "Women Leading Tech"
**Dr. Meera Patel** delivered a powerful address on the challenges and opportunities for women in technology leadership roles, sharing personal insights from her journey in academia and industry collaborations.

#### Technical Interview Preparation Workshop
A practical session coaching participants on technical interview strategies, common coding challenges, and effective communication skills.

### Afternoon Sessions

#### Leadership Workshop
**Neha Desai** conducted an interactive workshop on developing leadership skills specific to the technology industry, covering team management, technical communication, and career advancement strategies.

#### Tech Entrepreneurship Panel
A discussion featuring founders and early-stage investors sharing insights on starting technology ventures, securing funding, and building successful tech products.

#### Closing Ceremony
The event concluded with a ceremony recognizing outstanding community contributors and announcing upcoming GDG WOW Pune initiatives.

## Speaker Profiles

### Dr. Priya Sharma
**AI Research Lead, Google**
Dr. Sharma has over 15 years of experience in artificial intelligence research, with a focus on natural language processing and computer vision. She leads a team at Google developing next-generation AI technologies with built-in ethical safeguards.

### Anjali Mehta
**CTO, CloudTech Solutions**
With extensive experience architecting enterprise cloud solutions, Anjali specializes in helping organizations transition to cloud-native architectures. She is a Google Cloud certified architect and frequent speaker at technology conferences.

### Neha Desai
**Founder & CEO, TechWomen Initiative**
After a successful career as a software engineer at major tech companies, Neha founded the TechWomen Initiative to help women advance in technology careers. Her organization has mentored over 5,000 women technologists globally.

### Vikram Singh
**Engineering Director, Google**
Vikram leads the Flutter development team at Google and has been instrumental in advancing cross-platform application development. He is passionate about developer experience and building tools that make coding more accessible.

### Dr. Meera Patel
**Professor of Computer Science**
A renowned academic with significant industry experience, Dr. Patel's research focuses on human-computer interaction and computer science education. She has published extensively on creating inclusive learning environments for computer science.

### Kiran Kumar
**Senior Developer Advocate**
Kiran specializes in Firebase implementations and has helped countless developers integrate Google's development platform into their applications. He is known for his clear explanations and practical, real-world examples.
