# GDG WOW Pune Experience

The Google Developer Group Women in Tech (GDG WOW) Pune events bring together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from across the region. These gatherings provide excellent opportunities for networking, learning, and sharing knowledge about the latest Google technologies and industry best practices.

## Highlights

- Interactive workshops and hands-on sessions
- Inspiring talks by industry leaders
- Networking opportunities with like-minded professionals
- Supportive community for women in technology

Join us at our next event to expand your skills, make meaningful connections, and be part of our vibrant tech community!
