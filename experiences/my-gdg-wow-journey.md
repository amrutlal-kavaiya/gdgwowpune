# My Journey with GDG Women of Wearables (WOW) Pune

## Introduction

Hello! I'm excited to share my personal journey and experiences with Google Developer Group Women of Wearables (GDG WOW) Pune. This community has been instrumental in my growth as a developer and has provided me with invaluable opportunities to learn, connect, and contribute to the tech ecosystem in Pune.

## How I Got Started

My journey with GDG WOW began in early 2024 when I attended my first event - a workshop on Flutter for wearable applications. What immediately struck me was the welcoming and inclusive atmosphere. As someone who had previously felt intimidated at tech events, the supportive environment at GDG WOW made a world of difference.

## Key Learning Experiences

Throughout my involvement with GDG WOW Pune, I've had the opportunity to:

1. **Attend Technical Workshops**: From AR/VR development to IoT fundamentals, the hands-on workshops have expanded my technical skills significantly.

2. **Participate in Hackathons**: The collaborative problem-solving during the 24-hour WOW Hackathon pushed me to think creatively and develop solutions under pressure.

3. **Network with Industry Experts**: Meeting professionals from Google, local startups, and established tech companies has broadened my understanding of career pathways in technology.

4. **Volunteer and Lead**: After attending several events, I stepped up as a volunteer and eventually led a workshop on [specific technology], which boosted my confidence in public speaking and knowledge sharing.

## Impact on My Career

Being part of GDG WOW has tangibly impacted my professional growth:

- I gained the confidence to apply for roles I previously thought were out of reach
- The mentor connections I made helped me navigate career challenges
- The portfolio of projects I built during GDG events strengthened my resume
- The community support encouraged me to pursue further education in emerging technologies

## Why GDG WOW Matters

What makes GDG WOW Pune special is its focus on:

- Creating spaces where underrepresented groups in tech feel welcome
- Providing practical, hands-on learning experiences
- Building a supportive network that extends beyond just technical knowledge
- Encouraging members to step out of their comfort zones and grow

## Looking Forward

As I continue my journey with GDG WOW Pune, I'm excited to:

- Contribute more actively as a community organizer
- Mentor newcomers who remind me of myself when I first joined
- Propose and lead initiatives that address the specific needs of our local tech community
- Continue learning and growing alongside this amazing community

## Conclusion

GDG WOW Pune has been more than just a tech community for me—it's been a catalyst for personal and professional growth. I'm grateful for the friendships formed, the knowledge gained, and the opportunities discovered through this incredible group.

I encourage anyone interested in technology, regardless of their background or experience level, to join GDG WOW Pune. The supportive environment and valuable learning experiences make it truly worthwhile!
