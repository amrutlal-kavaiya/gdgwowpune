# AI AND ML SESSION

## My Experience at GDG WOW Pune AI/ML Workshop

### Event Overview

The GDG WOW Pune AI/ML workshop was a transformative experience that provided deep insights into artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies. Held on April 15, 2025, the full-day workshop attracted over 100 participants from diverse backgrounds.

### What I Learned

#### Morning Session: Foundations of AI

- **Introduction to AI Concepts**: Explored the differences between narrow AI, general AI, and the current state of AI technologies
- **Machine Learning Fundamentals**: Learned about supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning approaches
- **Neural Networks**: Gained understanding of how neural networks function as the foundation of deep learning
- **Ethical Considerations**: Discussed bias in AI, privacy concerns, and responsible AI development

#### Afternoon Session: Hands-on ML

- **Python for Data Science**: Refreshed Python skills with focus on data manipulation libraries
- **TensorFlow Workshop**: Built and trained a basic image classification model
- **Google Colab Environment**: Utilized cloud-based computing for ML projects without local setup
- **Model Evaluation**: Learned techniques for assessing model performance and avoiding overfitting

### Networking Opportunities

The workshop provided excellent opportunities to connect with:
- Industry professionals from leading tech companies
- Academic researchers exploring cutting-edge AI applications
- Fellow developers interested in implementing AI solutions
- Google Developer Experts specializing in ML technologies

### Key Takeaways

1. AI and ML are accessible technologies with the right learning approach
2. Practical implementation is crucial for cementing theoretical knowledge
3. Community support greatly accelerates the learning process
4. Ethical considerations should be integrated from the beginning of AI projects
5. Regular practice with tools like TensorFlow and PyTorch is essential

### Future Plans

This workshop has inspired me to:
- Continue exploring ML through online courses and resources
- Join the GDG Pune study group for ongoing learning
- Apply these technologies to solve real-world problems
- Share knowledge with others beginning their AI/ML journey
