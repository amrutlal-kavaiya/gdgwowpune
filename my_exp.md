# My Experience at GDG WOW Pune

## AI/ML Technologies We Learned

During the GDG Women in Tech Pune event, we had the opportunity to learn about several cutting-edge AI technologies:

### MCP (Mixture of Competent Professionals)
We learned how MCP architecture allows AI systems to route queries to specialized "expert" sub-networks, improving performance on diverse tasks. This approach helps models handle different domains more efficiently by activating only the relevant parts of the network for specific queries.

### RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
RAG was one of the most interesting topics we covered. This hybrid approach combines:
- Information retrieval from external knowledge sources
- Generation capabilities of large language models

This allows AI systems to provide more accurate, up-to-date responses by pulling in relevant information from databases or documents during the generation process.

### LLM Applications and Best Practices
We also explored:
- Prompt engineering techniques
- Fine-tuning strategies for specific domains
- Evaluation metrics for generative AI systems
- Ethical considerations when deploying AI solutions

### Hands-on Workshops
The practical sessions were invaluable, giving us experience with:
- Building simple RAG systems using open-source frameworks
- Evaluating model outputs for accuracy and relevance
- Addressing challenges like hallucinations and bias

## Networking and Community
Beyond the technical learning, connecting with other women in tech and industry professionals provided great insights into AI career paths and opportunities.

I'm excited to apply these technologies to solve real-world problems and continue exploring this rapidly evolving field!
