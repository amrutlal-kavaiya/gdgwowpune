# My Experience at GDG WOW Pune 2025

## Learning and Growth in Technology

We learnt about mcp, rag, vector databases, and other cutting-edge AI technologies during the incredible GDG WOW Pune 2025 event. The sessions were comprehensive and provided hands-on experience with these technologies.

### MCP (Mission Control Protocol)

The session on Mission Control Protocol demonstrated how distributed systems can communicate efficiently in real-time applications. The practical workshop helped us implement MCP in a sample application, showcasing its benefits for high-reliability systems.

### RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

One of the most enlightening sessions focused on RAG, a technique that enhances large language models by combining them with external knowledge retrieval systems. The presenter showed us how to:

- Set up a vector database for knowledge storage
- Implement efficient retrieval mechanisms
- Combine retrieved information with generative AI
- Fine-tune the system for domain-specific applications

### Vector Databases

The deep dive into vector databases was particularly valuable. We learned about:

- Different vector database solutions (Pinecone, Weaviate, FAISS)
- Embedding generation techniques
- Similarity search algorithms
- Scaling considerations for production environments

### Hands-on Workshops

The event wasn't just theoretical - we had multiple opportunities to apply what we learned:

1. Building a simple RAG application using Python and Hugging Face models
2. Setting up a vector database from scratch
3. Optimizing query performance in real-world scenarios
4. Deploying solutions to cloud environments

### Community Connections

Beyond the technical learning, the connections made with fellow developers and experts in the field were invaluable. The collaborative atmosphere encouraged sharing ideas and exploring potential solutions to common challenges.

### Looking Forward

The knowledge gained during GDG WOW Pune has already begun influencing my approach to projects, particularly in how I think about integrating AI capabilities into applications. I'm excited to continue exploring these technologies and to share what I've learned with my colleagues.

---

*This experience has been transformative for my technical growth, and I'm grateful to the organizers and presenters for making such complex topics accessible and engaging.*
