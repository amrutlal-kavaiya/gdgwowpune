# My Experience with Modern AI Technologies

## What We Learned

During our recent GDG WOW Pune event, we had the opportunity to explore some cutting-edge AI technologies. Here are the key concepts we learned about:

### MCP (Multi-modal Chain of Processing)

MCP is a powerful approach that allows AI systems to process and understand multiple forms of input simultaneously, such as:

- Text
- Images
- Audio
- Video

This multi-modal approach enables more comprehensive understanding and reasoning, mimicking how humans integrate different sensory inputs to form a complete picture of information.

Key benefits of MCP include:
- More robust understanding through cross-validation of information across modalities
- Ability to handle complex inputs that span multiple formats
- Enhanced context awareness and situational understanding

### RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

RAG combines the power of retrieval systems with generative AI to create more accurate and contextually relevant outputs. Here's how it works:

1. When a query is received, the system searches through a knowledge base to retrieve relevant information
2. This retrieved information is then provided as context to a generative model
3. The model generates a response that is grounded in the retrieved information

Key advantages of RAG include:
- Reduced hallucinations by grounding responses in retrieved knowledge
- Up-to-date information when connected to current knowledge bases
- Domain-specific expertise when specialized knowledge bases are used
- Greater transparency and source traceability
