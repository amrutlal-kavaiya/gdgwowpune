# My GDG WOW Pune Experience

## What I Learned

During the GDG WOW Pune event, I had the opportunity to learn about several cutting-edge AI technologies and approaches:

### MCP (Model, Context, Processing)
We learned about the MCP approach which helps in designing and implementing AI systems with a clear architecture:
- **Model**: The underlying AI models like LLMs and their capabilities
- **Context**: How to effectively manage context and prompts for better results
- **Processing**: Pre and post-processing techniques to enhance AI outputs

### RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
The session on RAG was particularly insightful. RAG combines:
- Information retrieval from external knowledge sources
- Generative AI to produce outputs based on the retrieved information
- This approach helps overcome hallucinations and provides more factual responses

### Prompt Engineering
We explored various prompt engineering techniques:
- Zero-shot, one-shot, and few-shot prompting
- Chain-of-thought prompting for complex reasoning
- System prompts vs. user prompts

### Fine-tuning and Evaluation
The workshop covered methods for:
- Fine-tuning foundation models for specific use cases
- Evaluating AI model performance with metrics like ROUGE, BLEU, etc.
- Human-in-the-loop feedback mechanisms

### Hands-on Experience
We participated in practical exercises that helped us apply these concepts in real-world scenarios. The collaborative environment allowed us to learn from peers and mentors alike.

## Future Application

I'm excited to apply these learnings to:
- Build more reliable AI applications using the RAG approach
- Implement effective prompting strategies for better results
- Consider the MCP framework when designing new AI solutions

This event has been incredibly valuable for my professional growth, and I look forward to participating in future GDG WOW Pune events.
