# AI Workshop with MCP Servers - GDG WOW Pune

## Introduction

Welcome to our recap of the exciting AI Workshop that took place at GDG WOW Pune! This hands-on session focused on implementing AI solutions using MCP (Multi-Cloud Platform) servers, bringing together developers, data scientists, and AI enthusiasts from across the region.

## Workshop Highlights

The workshop covered several key areas:

- Setting up MCP servers for AI workloads
- Optimizing infrastructure for machine learning models
- Deploying and scaling AI applications across multiple cloud environments
- Real-time inference with containerized AI models
- Best practices for MLOps on MCP architecture

## What We Learned

Participants gained valuable insights into how Multi-Cloud Platforms can enhance AI development workflows. The ability to distribute workloads across different cloud providers offers significant advantages for training large models and serving predictions at scale.

The hands-on portions allowed everyone to experience the power of these tools firsthand, with guided exercises on configuring server environments, optimizing for GPU acceleration, and implementing proper monitoring for AI systems.

## Community Collaboration

One of the most valuable aspects of the workshop was the collaboration between participants. Teams formed organically to solve challenges, and the room was buzzing with creative solutions and knowledge sharing.
