# My Experience at GDG WOW Pune 2024

## An Inspiring Journey of Tech and Empowerment

As I stepped into the venue for Google Developer Group Women of the World (GDG WOW) Pune 2024, I could feel the energy and excitement in the air. The event was a celebration of women in technology, and I was thrilled to be part of this empowering experience.

### Keynote Sessions That Sparked Innovation

The day began with a powerful keynote by Priya Sharma, a senior Google engineer, who shared her journey from a small-town girl to leading AI projects at Google. Her story of perseverance and innovation resonated deeply with everyone in the audience.

### Hands-on Workshops That Elevated Skills

#### Machine Learning Workshop
I participated in a hands-on machine learning workshop where we built a simple ML model using TensorFlow. The session was led by industry experts who made complex concepts accessible and engaging.

#### Cloud Computing Deep Dive
The Google Cloud Platform workshop opened my eyes to the possibilities of cloud computing. We deployed our first serverless application, and the sense of achievement was incredible!

### Networking and Building Connections

One of the most valuable aspects of GDG WOW was the networking opportunities. I met:
- Fellow developers from diverse backgrounds
- Industry leaders who shared valuable insights
- Mentors who offered guidance for career growth

### Key Takeaways

1. **Technical Growth**: Enhanced understanding of modern development tools and practices
2. **Community Support**: Experienced the strength of women supporting women in tech
3. **Career Inspiration**: Gained clarity on potential career paths in technology
4. **Networking**: Built connections that will last beyond the event

### Why GDG WOW Matters

GDG WOW Pune is more than just a tech conference – it's a movement that:
- Empowers women to pursue careers in technology
- Creates safe spaces for learning and sharing
- Builds bridges between different levels of expertise
- Fosters a supportive community

### Looking Forward

As I reflect on my experience at GDG WOW Pune 2024, I'm filled with gratitude and motivation. The event has not only upgraded my technical skills but also connected me with a community that celebrates diversity and encourages growth.

I'm already looking forward to applying what I learned and contributing back to the community. GDG WOW Pune has shown me that together, we can break barriers and create a more inclusive tech ecosystem.
