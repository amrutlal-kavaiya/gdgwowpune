# My Experience at GDG WOW Pune 2025

## A Journey of Inspiration, Learning, and Community

![GDG WOW Pune 2025](https://gdg.community.dev/assets/logo.svg)

### Introduction

The Google Developer Group Women of Wonders (GDG WOW) Pune 2025 event was held on March 12-13, 2025, at the Hyatt Regency Pune. This two-day conference brought together women technologists, allies, and industry leaders from across India and beyond. As a first-time attendee, I was blown away by the depth of technical content, the inclusive atmosphere, and the powerful networking opportunities.

### Day 1: Technical Deep Dives

#### Opening Keynote: "AI for All"

The event kicked off with an inspiring keynote by Sarah Chen, Director of Google AI Research, who highlighted how AI can be made more accessible and inclusive. Her demonstration of the latest advancements in Google's generative AI models was mind-blowing, particularly the real-time translation and accessibility features that could help bridge digital divides.

#### Web Development Track

I primarily followed the Web Development track on Day 1, where the session on "Building Progressive Web Apps with Project Fugu APIs" by Divya Mohan was particularly enlightening. Learning how to leverage advanced browser capabilities to create app-like experiences without requiring actual app installations opened my eyes to new possibilities for my current project.

#### Hands-on Workshop: Flutter for Web

The hands-on Flutter for Web workshop guided by Neha Singh was exceptionally well-organized. Starting from the basics, we quickly moved to building a fully functional dashboard application with offline capabilities. What impressed me most was how the instructors made time to address individual questions, ensuring no one was left behind.

### Day 2: Career Growth and Community Building

#### Panel Discussion: "Breaking the Glass Ceiling"

A highlight of Day 2 was the panel discussion featuring women tech leaders from companies like Google, Microsoft, and local startups. Hearing their honest stories about overcoming challenges in male-dominated environments was both motivating and practical. The actionable advice about negotiation tactics and finding mentors will definitely shape my approach to career development.

#### Coding Competition

The afternoon coding competition was intense but incredibly fun! Teams had four hours to build an application addressing a sustainability challenge using Google technologies. Even though our team didn't win, the experience of rapidly brainstorming, planning, and executing a solution under pressure was invaluable.

#### Networking Mixer

The evening networking mixer deserves special mention. Unlike typical awkward networking events, the organizers facilitated meaningful connections through structured activities that matched people based on interests and expertise. I connected with three potential mentors and several peers working on similar challenges.

### Technical Highlights

Throughout the conference, several technologies stood out:

1. **TensorFlow Lite** - Demonstrations showed how on-device machine learning can enhance privacy while enabling powerful features
2. **Firebase Flutter Extensions** - New extensions revealed at the event drastically simplified authentication and real-time database operations
3. **Chrome DevTools** - Advanced debugging techniques showcased during performance optimization sessions will likely save me countless hours of work

### Community Impact

What truly set GDG WOW Pune apart was its focus on community building. The conference wasn't just about technical knowledge—it emphasized creating lasting connections and support networks. The "Community Project Showcase" highlighted incredible initiatives led by women developers across Maharashtra, from educational apps for rural students to healthcare solutions for underserved populations.

### Personal Growth

Attending this event pushed me out of my comfort zone in the best possible way. I presented a lightning talk on my recent project, received valuable feedback, and gained confidence in my technical abilities. The supportive environment made it safe to ask questions and admit knowledge gaps without fear of judgment.

### Conclusion

The GDG WOW Pune 2025 was more than just a technical conference—it was a transformative experience that reinvigorated my passion for technology and community. The combination of cutting-edge technical content, career guidance, and genuine community building created an unforgettable experience.

I'm already looking forward to GDG WOW 2026, and in the meantime, I'm excited to attend local GDG meetups to continue learning and contributing to this incredible community.
