# My Journey with GDG WOW Pune

## Introduction

Hello everyone! I'm excited to share my experience with Google Developer Group Women of Wonders (WOW) Pune. This community has been an integral part of my growth as a developer and has provided me with countless opportunities to learn, network, and contribute to the tech ecosystem.

## How I Got Started

My journey with GDG WOW Pune began when I attended my first DevFest in 2024. The inclusive environment and the diversity of topics covered immediately caught my attention. From that moment on, I knew I wanted to be an active part of this community.

## What I've Learned

Being part of GDG WOW Pune has helped me:

1. **Expand my technical knowledge**: Through various workshops, codelab sessions, and tech talks, I've gained insights into Google technologies like Flutter, Firebase, TensorFlow, and more.

2. **Build my network**: I've connected with like-minded developers, mentors, and industry experts who have guided me through different stages of my career.

3. **Develop leadership skills**: Volunteering for events has taught me how to work in teams, manage time effectively, and take initiative.

## My Favorite Events

- **International Women's Day Celebration (2024)**: A day full of inspiring talks from women leaders in tech, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities.
  
- **Flutter Study Jam**: A series of sessions that helped me understand Flutter framework in depth and build my first cross-platform app.

- **Cloud Next Extended**: An opportunity to explore cloud technologies and their practical applications in real-world scenarios.

## How I Contribute

I believe in giving back to the community that has helped me grow. Here's how I contribute:

- **Mentoring newcomers**: Helping those who are just starting their journey in tech.
  
- **Content creation**: Writing articles and creating tutorials on various tech topics.
  
- **Event organization**: Assisting in organizing and managing community events.

## Future Goals

Moving forward, I aim to:

1. Lead more technical workshops for women beginning their journey in technology.
2. Contribute to open-source projects under GDG WOW Pune.
3. Expand our community outreach to include more students and professionals.

## Conclusion

GDG WOW Pune is more than just a tech community; it's a family of passionate individuals working together to create an inclusive tech ecosystem. I'm grateful for the opportunities and look forward to many more years of learning and growth with this amazing community.
