# My Amazing Experience at GDG WOW DevFest Pune 2024

## Introduction

Last weekend, I had the incredible opportunity to attend the Google Developer Group (GDG) Women of WOW (WOW) DevFest in Pune. As someone passionate about technology and community building, this event exceeded all my expectations. Here's a recap of my experience and the valuable insights I gained.

## The Event Setting

The event was held at a modern tech campus in the heart of Pune, creating the perfect atmosphere for innovation and collaboration. From the moment I walked in, I was greeted by enthusiastic volunteers and a vibrant crowd of tech enthusiasts, developers, and industry professionals.

## Technical Workshops

The DevFest featured multiple tracks of workshops catering to different skill levels and interests:

1. **Introduction to TensorFlow** - I attended this session where we built a simple image classification model. The hands-on approach made complex concepts accessible even to beginners.

2. **Flutter App Development** - This workshop demonstrated how to build cross-platform apps with Flutter. We created a functional app in just a few hours!

3. **Cloud Computing with Google Cloud Platform** - This session explored various GCP services and included practical demos of deploying applications.

## Inspiring Keynotes

The keynote speakers were truly inspiring. I was particularly moved by talks focusing on:

- Women in tech leadership roles and breaking barriers
- The future of AI and machine learning
- Building inclusive developer communities
- Sustainable technology practices

## Networking Opportunities

One of the best aspects of the event was connecting with like-minded individuals. I exchanged ideas with developers from various backgrounds, discussed potential collaborations, and even found a mentor who's willing to guide me in my career path.

## Hackathon Experience

The 6-hour mini-hackathon was exhilarating! Our team built a solution addressing local transportation challenges using Google Maps API. Although we didn't win, the experience of ideating and implementing a solution under time constraints was invaluable.

## Key Takeaways

1. The importance of community in tech growth
2. Hands-on learning is more effective than theoretical knowledge alone
3. Diversity brings richer perspectives to problem-solving
4. Technology can be a powerful tool for social good

## Looking Forward

This DevFest has motivated me to become more active in the GDG WOW Pune community. I plan to attend future meetups, contribute to community projects, and perhaps even speak at an upcoming event.

If you're hesitating about attending tech community events, especially as a beginner or someone from an underrepresented group in tech, I strongly encourage you to take the leap. The GDG WOW community is welcoming, supportive, and an excellent platform for growth.

Here's to many more enriching tech community experiences!
