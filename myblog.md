# My GDGoC WOW Pune Experience

## Introduction

Hello everyone! I'm excited to share my incredible experience at the Google Developer Group Women of Wonders (GDG WOW) event held in Pune. This was part of the Google Developer Group Open Community (GDGoC) initiative, which aims to foster an inclusive and diverse tech community across India.

## The Event Overview

The GDG WOW Pune event took place on April 15, 2025, at the Pune Tech Hub. It was a full-day event packed with insightful sessions, hands-on workshops, networking opportunities, and inspiring stories from women in technology. The theme of the event was "Innovation Without Boundaries," highlighting the importance of breaking barriers in the tech industry.

## Key Highlights

### Opening Keynote

The day began with an inspiring keynote by a senior engineering leader from Google, who shared her journey in tech and the importance of community support for women developers. Her story of overcoming challenges and breaking stereotypes set an empowering tone for the day.

### Technical Workshops

The workshops were the most valuable part of my experience:

1. **Flutter Development Workshop**: A hands-on session where we built a cross-platform application from scratch. The mentors were patient and knowledgeable, guiding beginners and experienced developers alike.

2. **Cloud Computing with Google Cloud Platform**: This workshop introduced us to GCP services and demonstrated how to deploy applications to the cloud. We worked on practical examples that showcased the power and flexibility of cloud infrastructure.

3. **Machine Learning Fundamentals**: An introduction to TensorFlow and practical ML applications. We trained a simple model during the session, which was incredibly exciting!

### Panel Discussion: "Breaking Barriers in Tech"

A diverse panel of women leaders from various tech companies in Pune discussed challenges they've faced and strategies they've employed to succeed in the industry. The candid sharing of experiences and practical advice was both relatable and actionable.

### Networking Session

The structured networking activities helped everyone connect meaningfully. I met many talented developers and professionals from various backgrounds, and we exchanged ideas, contacts, and potential collaboration opportunities.

## Personal Takeaways

### Knowledge Enhancement

I gained significant technical knowledge, particularly in Flutter development, which I plan to apply to my current projects. The practical, hands-on approach of the workshops made complex concepts accessible.

### Community Connection

The most valuable aspect was connecting with like-minded individuals who are passionate about technology. The GDG WOW community is supportive, encouraging, and motivating.

### Increased Confidence

Seeing successful women in technology share their journeys and challenges has boosted my confidence. Their stories reminded me that perseverance and continuous learning are key to success in this field.

### Mentorship Opportunities

I connected with potential mentors who offered guidance for my career development. This kind of support network is invaluable for personal and professional growth.

## Future Participation

This event has inspired me to become more active in the GDG community. I plan to:

- Attend upcoming GDG events
- Contribute to community projects
- Eventually share my knowledge by speaking at future events
- Support and mentor newcomers to the community

## Conclusion

The GDGoC WOW Pune event was not just a technical conference but a transformative experience that provided learning, networking, and inspiration. It demonstrated the power of community and the impact of bringing diverse perspectives to technology.

I'm grateful to the organizers, speakers, and fellow participants for making this event so meaningful. If you haven't attended a GDG WOW event yet, I highly recommend it – regardless of your gender or technical background. These inclusive spaces are where innovation truly thrives!
