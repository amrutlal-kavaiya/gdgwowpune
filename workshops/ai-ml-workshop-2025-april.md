# AI/ML Workshop - GDG WOW Pune April 2025

## Event Details

**Date:** April 20, 2025  
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM IST  
**Venue:** Pune Tech Hub, Hinjawadi Phase 2  
**Registration:** [Register here](https://gdg.community.dev/events/gdgwowpune-april-2025)

## About the Workshop

This hands-on workshop focuses on building, training, and deploying AI/ML models using Google Cloud Platform. Participants will gain practical experience with TensorFlow, Vertex AI, and BigQuery ML to solve real-world machine learning problems.

## Workshop Agenda

### Morning Session (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM)

- **10:30 - 11:15** - Getting Started with Google Cloud for AI/ML
- **11:15 - 11:30** - Coffee Break
- **11:30 - 1:00** - Hands-on Lab: Data Preparation and Feature Engineering

### Lunch Break (1:00 PM - 2:00 PM)
- Networking lunch provided for all participants

### Afternoon Session (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
- **2:00 - 3:30** - Hands-on Lab: Model Training with TensorFlow and Vertex AI
- **3:30 - 3:45** - Coffee Break
- **3:45 - 4:30** - Model Deployment and Inference
- **4:30 - 5:00** - Q&A and Closing Remarks
