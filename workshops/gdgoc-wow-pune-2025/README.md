# GDG OC Wow Pune Workshop 2025

## Workshop Overview

The GDG Orange County and GDG WOW Pune collaborative workshop is a cross-community initiative designed to share knowledge, foster collaboration, and build connections between tech communities in Orange County, California and Pune, India.

## Event Details

- **Date**: May 15-16, 2025
- **Location**: 
  - In-person: Tech Hub Pune, MG Road, Pune, Maharashtra, India
  - Virtual: Livestreamed for global audience
- **Duration**: 2-day immersive workshop

## Workshop Topics

- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with Google's Gemini
- Flutter for cross-platform app development
- Google Cloud Platform fundamentals and implementation
- Career development for women in technology
- Collaborative problem-solving through hackathon
